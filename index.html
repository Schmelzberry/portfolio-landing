    Making wort
  • 1. Mash in your grist.
  • 2. Rest mash for x amount of minutes.
  • 3. Vorlauf aka recirculate your mash to ensure homegeneity.
  • 4. Lauter all of that wonderful extract to a predetermined volume.
  • 5. Boil your wort, and carefully add the hops at correct times.
  • 6. Transfer after boil is complete to the whirlpool.
  • 7."knock out" your beer to the fermentation vessel, pitching yeast at the desired temperature.
  • 8.After fermentation is complete, drink that bad boi and enjoy the fruits of your labor!
