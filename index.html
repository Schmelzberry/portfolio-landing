    My Favorite Hobby, Explained

I love talking about beer, below there's a little guide to making it!

  • Mash in grist or crushed grain by mixing with hot liquor.
  • Rest mash for some minutes(tends to vary on beer style).
  • Vorlauf aka recirculate your mash to ensure homegeneity.
  • Lauter all of that wonderful extract to a predetermined volume.
  • Boil your wort, and carefully add the hops at correct times. Dilute beer if performing "high gravity" brewing
  • Transfer after boil is complete to the whirlpool.
  • "knock out" your beer to the fermentation vessel, pitching yeast at the desired temperature.
  • After fermentation is complete, and assuming you're of age, drink that bad boi and enjoy the fruits of your labor!
