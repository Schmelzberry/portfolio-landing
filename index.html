My Favorite Hobby

I love talking about beer, below is a little guide to making it!

  1. Mash in grist or crushed grain by mixing with hot liquor.
  2. Rest mash for some minutes(tends to vary on beer style).
  3. Vorlauf aka recirculate your mash to ensure homegeneity.
  4. Lauter all of that wonderful extract to a predetermined volume.
  5. Boil your wort, and carefully add the hops at correct times. Dilute beer if performing "high gravity" brewing
  6. Transfer after boil is complete to the whirlpool.
  7. "knock out" your beer to the fermentation vessel, pitching yeast at the desired beer temperature.
  8. After fermentation is complete, and assuming you're of age, drink that bad boi and enjoy the fruits of your labor!
